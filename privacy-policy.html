Privacy Policy

Last updated: Wednesday, February 25, 2026

Salat Time respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Salat Time mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Salat Time is a free mobile application that provides Islamic features such as:

  • Prayer times (Salat times)
  • Prayer notifications
  • Qiblah direction
  • Tasbih counter
  • Quran reading and listening

The App does not require user registration or account creation, and does not collect or store personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Location history

Salat Time can be used fully without providing any personal data.

2.2 Permissions Used

  • Location Permission:
    • Used to calculate accurate prayer times
    • Used to determine the correct Qiblah direction
    • Location data is processed locally on your device
    • We do not store, track, or transmit your location
  • Notification Permission:
    • Used to send prayer time reminders and alerts
    • Notifications are generated locally
    • No notification data is collected or stored

3. Third-Party Services

Salat Time relies on third-party services to provide accurate Islamic content.

3.1 Prayer Times API

  • Prayer times are provided using the Aladhan API.
  • Used only to retrieve prayer time data
  • No personal data is sent to or stored by the App

3.2 Quran Content API

  • Quran text and audio are provided by the Quran API.
  • Used only to fetch Quran content
  • No user data is transmitted

3.3 Mobile Advertising

  • Salat Time displays ads to keep the app free for users.
  • Ads may be provided by third-party ad networks (e.g. Google AdMob)
  • These services may collect non-personal, anonymized data such as:
    • Ad interactions
    • Device type
    • General usage statistics
  • This data collection is governed by the privacy policies of the respective advertising providers. We do not have access to or control over data collected by ad networks.

4. Data Storage

  • Salat Time does not store any personal data on servers
  • Any temporary data (such as prayer time calculations or tasbih counters) is stored locally on your device only
  • Clearing the app or uninstalling it removes all local data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data because no personal data is collected.

6. Children’s Privacy

Salat Time does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Because no accounts or personal data are collected, the App is safe for use by all age groups.

7. Security

Although Salat Time does not collect personal data, we take reasonable measures to ensure the App operates securely and responsibly.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the updated date will be revised accordingly. We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
